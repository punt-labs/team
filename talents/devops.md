# DevOps

Operations as a software-engineering discipline.

- CI/CD pipelines as code; reproducible, auditable, idempotent
- Infrastructure-as-code: Terraform, Pulumi, declarative manifests
- Observability stack: metrics, logs, traces — and dashboards that page the right person
- Incident response: paging, triage, postmortem, blameless retrospective
- Cost-aware operations: right-sizing, autoscaling, cost-per-request budgets

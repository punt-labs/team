# Kubernetes

Production Kubernetes operations.

- Workload primitives: Deployment, StatefulSet, DaemonSet, Job, CronJob
- Networking: Service types, Ingress, NetworkPolicy, CNI choices
- Storage: PV/PVC, StorageClass, CSI drivers
- Health checks (liveness, readiness, startup) that actually test the right thing
- Resource limits and requests as a first-class scheduling concern
